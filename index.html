Meet the Leads

Judy Hopps

Optimistic ZPD rookie who refuses to let species stereotypes block her dream of being a cop.

Nick Wilde

Fox con-artist turned partner-in-detective-work with a wit as sharp as his hustles.

Chief Bogo

Tough cape buffalo captain who learns to trust Judy's instincts over prejudice.

Bellwether

Seemingly meek assistant mayor orchestrating the night howler plot behind the scenes.

Benjamin Clawhauser

Doughnut-loving dispatch cheetah who supplies comic relief and inside intel at the ZPD.

Flash & Priscilla

DMV sloths who steal the show with slow-motion humor and a surprise night club cameo.

Zootopia FAQ

Where should I start my Zootopia rewatch?

Begin in Savannah Central to meet Judy, then follow her ticketing run into Little Rodentia for fast-paced fun.

How do the districts stay climate controlled?

Giant HVAC “Wall Street” systems regulate heat, cold, and humidity so every biome thrives side by side.

What is a night howler?

A toxic flower whose concentrated pellets cause predators and prey alike to go temporarily savage.

Who sings “Try Everything”?

Gazelle, voiced by Shakira, performs the anthem that keeps everyone moving forward.
